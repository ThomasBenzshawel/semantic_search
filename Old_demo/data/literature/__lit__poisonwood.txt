The Poisonwood Bible, published in 1998 by American author Barbara Kingsolver, is a novel that follows the Price family as they embark on a mission trip to the Belgian Congo in 1959. Led by the zealous and stubborn Nathan Price, the family grapples with cultural clashes, political turmoil, and personal crises.
