Changes: A Love Story, published in 1991 by Ghanaian author Ama Ata Aidoo, is a novel that explores the complexities of love and marriage in a changing society. The novel follows the life of Esi Sekyi, a modern Ghanaian woman who is caught between tradition and progress as she navigates her relationships with two very different men. Through Esi’s story, Aidoo examines the impact of colonialism and the struggle for women’s rights in post-independence Ghana.
