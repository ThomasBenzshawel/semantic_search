Will Grayson, Will Grayson is a 2010 coming-of-age young adult novel by John Green and David Levithan. The story is narrated by the two title characters of the same name in alternating chapters: Green as Evanston Will and Levithan as Naperville Will. Both Wills cross paths one life-changing night and are set on a course toward self-discovery, truth, and love. Will Grayson, Will Grayson won a Stonewall Book Award and was the first young adult LGBTQ+ novel to debut on the New York Times bestseller list.
