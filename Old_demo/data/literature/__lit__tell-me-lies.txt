Tell Me Lies, Carola Lovering’s 2018 coming-of-age novel, follows the toxic, intoxicating love affair between Lucy Albright and Stephen DeMarco. The book alternates narrative points of view throughout their intensely sexual, disastrous relationship, illustrating the tension between their individual desires and revealing the profound ways their early lives have shaped them. Lauded as a perfect beach read by publications from Vogue and Cosmopolitan to US Weekly, the story debuted as a Hulu Original Series in 2022. Lovering’s other works include Too Good To Be True (2004), Can’t Look Away (2022), and Bye, Baby (2024).
