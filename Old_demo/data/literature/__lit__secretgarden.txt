The Secret Garden, a novel by Frances Hodgson Burnett, was first published in 1911. It tells the story of Mary Lennox, a young girl who is sent to live with her uncle in England after her parents die in India. Mary is a spoiled and sickly child, but she begins to thrive after she discovers a hidden garden on her uncle's estate.
