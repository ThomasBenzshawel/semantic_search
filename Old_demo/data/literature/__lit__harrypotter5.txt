Harry Potter and The Order of the Phoenix, the fifth book in J. K. Rowling’s Harry Potter series, follows Harry’s fifth year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Harry is frustrated by the Ministry of Magic’s refusal to acknowledge the return of the evil Lord Voldemort and the threat he poses. As a result, Harry forms a secret society, Dumbledore’s Army, to teach his fellow students defensive magic. The novel was published in 2003 and adapted into a film in 2007.
