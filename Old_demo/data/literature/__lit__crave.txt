Crave is a 2020 paranormal romance novel by Tracy Wolff, and the first book in the six-part Crave series. It centers on Grace Foster, a recently orphaned teenager who finds herself unknowingly dropped into an intense, frightening, and thrilling world of vampires, witches, and other supernatural beings at the remote Katmere Academy in Alaska. The #1 New York Times bestseller follows Grace as she navigates desire, grief, self-discovery, and typical teenage angst, all while dealing with earthquakes, near-fatal incidents, and dark forces that may or may not want her dead.
