Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire is the fourth book in J. K. Rowling’s Harry Potter series. It was published in 2000 and follows Harry’s fourth year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry as he unexpectedly becomes a competitor in the Triwizard Tournament, a dangerous magical competition. As dark forces stir, Harry faces formidable challenges and uncovers a sinister plot. Packed with magical duels and unexpected twists, the novel explores the challenges of adolescence and sets the stage for the escalating conflict in the wizarding world. The Goblet of Fire was adapted into a film in 2005.
