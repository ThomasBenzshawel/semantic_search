The Light in the Forest, published in 1953, is an historical novel by Conrad Richter. It tells the story of True Son, a white boy who was kidnapped and raised by Native Americans but is forced to return to his family as a young teenager. The novel is set in the 1760s and explores themes of identity, belonging, and the impact of violence and trauma on individuals and communities.
