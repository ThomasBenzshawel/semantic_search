Heartstopper began as a webcomic by Alice Oseman that went viral, gaining more than 50 million views. Oseman has been publishing Heartstopper as a six-volume series of graphic novels since 2019, the final volume of which is forthcoming. Heartstopper: Volume 1, is an LGBTQ+, young adult romance that tells the heartwarming love story of Charlie Spring and Nick Nelson and explores themes of gender and sexual identity, bullying, and self-discovery. The series has sold more than one million print copies and was adapted by Netflix into an immensely popular show, its second season garnering over six million viewers.
