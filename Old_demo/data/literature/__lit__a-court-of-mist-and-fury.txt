A Court of Mist and Fury by Sarah J. Maas is the second book in the five-part A Court of Thorns and Roses series. It follows the story of Feyre, a human huntress who was reborn as a faerie, and Rhysand, High Lord of the Night Court. A blend of romance, fantasy, and adventure, the novel was a #1 New York Times bestseller that presents an epic saga from a contemporary female perspective. The story won a 2016 Goodreads Choice Award and made Pop Sugar’s Best YA Books of 2016 list.
