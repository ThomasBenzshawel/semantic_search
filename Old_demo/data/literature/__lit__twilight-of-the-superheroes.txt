“Twilight of the Superheroes” by Deborah Eisenberg is a short story that weaves together the introspections of two men, Nathaniel and his uncle Lucien,  as they each individually reflect on their lives in post-9/11 New York City. Published in 2006, it reflects the anxieties and uncertainties of the time. Eisenberg delves into the complexities of human relationships and the impact of geopolitical events on personal lives. The story addresses themes of love, loss, and the search for meaning in a rapidly changing world.
