The Land is a young adult novel by American author Mildred D. Taylor that was first published in 2001. It is the sixth book in the Logan Family Saga, which follows the Logans, a Black family living in Mississippi during the Jim Crow era. As they strive for economic independence on their own piece of land, their family faces racial prejudice and economic challenges.
