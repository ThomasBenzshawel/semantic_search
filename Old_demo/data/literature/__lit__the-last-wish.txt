The Last Wish: Introducing the Witcher is the first installment of Adrzej Sapkowski’s fantasy book series, The Witcher. Translated to English from its original Polish by Danusia Stok, The Last Wish tells the story of witcher Geralt of Rivia, as he battles monsters, breaks curses, finds love, and begins a journey of self-reflection. Geralt’s adventures have fascinated audiences around the world and the stories have been adapted into a popular video game series and a Netflix show.
