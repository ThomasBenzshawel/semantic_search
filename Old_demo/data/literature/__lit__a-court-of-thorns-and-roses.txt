A Court of Thorns and Roses, Sarah J. Mass’s epic 2015 fantasy novel, tells the story of the human huntress Feyre and the faerie lord Tamlin. Blending romance, adventure, and fairy tale, Maas captured the hearts and imaginations of teenage and adult readers. A Court of Thorns and Roses earned a spot on The New York Times Best Seller list, as well as best-of recognition from Bustle, Business Insider, Buzz Feed, and Pop Sugar. The fantasy launched a five-book series and has been optioned as a TV series by Hulu.
