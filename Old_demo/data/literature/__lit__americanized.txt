Americanized: Rebel Without a Green Card is Sara Saedi’s memoir of growing up as an undocumented Iranian immigrant in California. Published in 2018 and written in the context of the Trump administration’s anti-immigrant and anti-Muslim rhetoric, the book tells the story of a teenager living a typical American life while at risk of deportation. Americanized received widespread critical praise for its humor and accessibility. Saedi is a writer for film and television and the author of the Never Ever young adult fantasy romance series.
