Swedish environmental activist Greta Thunberg captured the world’s attention in 2018 when, at the age of 15, she went on strike from school to protest government inaction in the face of climate change. Since then, she has become a renowned climate activist and has addressed the United Nations and the Davos Economic Forum about global warming and the climate crisis. No One Is Too Small to Make a Difference, published in 2019, features a collection her speeches and highlights her blunt, direct signature style. The bookseller Waterstones named Thunberg Author of the Year for 2019.
