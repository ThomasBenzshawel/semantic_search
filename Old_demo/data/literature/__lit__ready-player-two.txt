Ready Player Two is a science fiction novel by Ernest Cline, published in 2020 as a sequel to his 2011 debut novel, Ready Player One. Set in a near-future virtual reality world, the story follows Wade Watts as he discovers a new technology that challenges the boundaries of the OASIS, the immersive digital universe. As Wade and his friends embark on a high-stakes quest, the narrative explores themes of technology, friendship, and the consequences of unchecked power in a world dominated by virtual reality.
