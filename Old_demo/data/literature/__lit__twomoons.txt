Walk Two Moons, published in 1994 by Sharon Creech, is a young adult novel that tells the story of thirteen-year-old Salamanca Tree Hiddle, who embarks on a cross-country road trip with her eccentric grandparents in order to find her missing mother. Along the way, Sal tells her grandparents the story of her friend Phoebe Winterbottom, whose mother also disappeared.
