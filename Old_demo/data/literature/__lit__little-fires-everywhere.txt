Little Fires Everywhere, a novel by Celeste Ng, was published in 2017. The novel is set in the late 1990s in Shaker Heights, Ohio, a meticulously planned and ordered community. The story follows the Richardson family and their interactions with Mia Warren, a single mother and artist, and her daughter, Pearl. The novel explores themes of motherhood, race, and identity. In 2020, a television adaptation of the novel starring Reese Witherspoon and Kerry Washington was released on Hulu.
