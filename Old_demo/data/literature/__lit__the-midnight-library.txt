Published in 2020, The Midnight Library is a metaphysical fantasy novel by Matt Haig about a woman who visits a library between life and death where she can visit infinite alternate versions of her life. The novel grapples with the corrosive nature of regret and despair and offers a hopeful view of the infinite possibilities contained within a single life.
