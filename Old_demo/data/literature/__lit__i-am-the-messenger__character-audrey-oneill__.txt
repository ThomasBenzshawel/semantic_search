Audrey, the love of Ed’s life, is a smart woman with an elfin frame. Audrey’s dainty bone structure and lively spirit have captivated Ed since their childhood. She has an unearthly quality emphasized by her evasiveness with Ed. Audrey’s delicate vulnerability first aroused Ed’s protectiveness when he watched the abuse she endured from those responsible for her. Over time, she has come to rely on their deep connection, which she prefers to view as a friendship rather than love. The distinction is all-important to her sense of safety. Ed uses dance to break through her protective walls. As he holds her in a loving embrace and they move together to the music, she experiences the true intimacy that a sexual relationship with Ed would be. Audrey realizes that Ed’s love is the safest place in her world.
