Across Five Aprils is a historical novel by Irene Hunt that was first published in 1964. The story follows Jethro Creighton, a young farm boy in Illinois, as he grapples with the impact of the Civil War on his family and community. Jethro's coming-of-age journey unfolds against the backdrop of divided loyalties, personal sacrifices, and the challenges of wartime.
