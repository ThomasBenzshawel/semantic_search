Published in 2015, Everything, Everything is a young adult novel about eighteen-year-old Madeline Whittier, who finds herself missing out on feeling truly alive as a result of a life-threatening immunodeficiency condition. It is the debut novel by Jamaican American writer Nicola Yoon. In the novel, Maddy finds love, but this strains her relationship with her mother. Everything, Everything was adapted into a film that was released in 2017.
