Eleanor & Park, a young adult novel by Rainbow Rowell, was published in 2013. The novel tells the story of two misfit teenagers, Eleanor and Park, who fall in love over the course of a school year in 1986. Eleanor, with her vibrant red hair and troubled home life, stands out in her new school. Park, a half-Korean comic book enthusiast, initially resents her presence but eventually they bond over music and comics.
