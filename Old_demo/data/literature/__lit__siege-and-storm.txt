Siege and Storm is the second book in Leigh Bardugo’s Shadow and Bone trilogy. It is set in Bardugo’s Grishaverse, a magical world in which Alina Starkov must use her powers as the Sun Summoner to counter the Darkling’s threats of destruction. Siege and Storm was first published in June 2013 and quickly became a New York Times Best Seller. Along with Bardugo’s other Grishaverse titles, Siege and Storm is now the subject of a popular Netflix series.
